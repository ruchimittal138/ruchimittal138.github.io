About
Me

"Bridging cutting-edge AI research with real-world enterprise impact."

I hold a PhD in Computer Science from the University of Delhi and have spent over 16 years at the intersection of academia and industry — specialising in Generative AI, Large Language Models, NLP, computer vision, and full-stack development.

As Principal Data Scientist at Iconic Data (Japan), I architect multi-agent AI ecosystems and collaborate directly with CXOs to translate strategic goals into intelligent solutions. I am also a Learning Facilitator at Emeritus, delivering AI/ML courses for IITs and Imperial Business School, London.

Recognised as a Subject Matter Expert in AI/ML, I have authored 50+ papers in SCI, ABDC, and Scopus-indexed journals, hold 2 patents, and have mentored multinational teams across industries and continents.
